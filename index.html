Diphlu River Lodge, Assam

This hideaway near Kaziranga is a treat for all parents as it doesn’t have any TVs! Yup, that’s right! But then your child won’t need one when he discovers the amount of fun and adventure being offered here. With bonfire and camping, you can spend quality time with your loved ones at one of the best resorts in India for family vacation. Instead, kids can spend their time on nature walks, dolphin boat safaris, tea garden visits and other jungle activities.

Location: Kaziranga National Park Kuthuri,, Nagaon, Assam 782136

Activities For Children: Nature walks, dolphin boat safaris, tea garden visits, jungle activities, bonfire & music

Starting Price: INR 3,000/- per night

Google Rating: 4.7/5 | TripAdvisor Rating: 4.5/5

Amanvana, Karnataka

A serene hideout in the hills, Amanvana is located along the banks of River Cauvery in Coorg and has private bungalows and skylit bathrooms. One of the best resorts in India for family, Amanvana has all the right ingredients to provide you with a fun and frolic family holiday.

Location: No 21, Guddehosur, Coorg, Kushalnagar 571234

Activities For Children: Barbecue by the water, film at the amphitheatre, trekking, bird sanctuaries

Starting Price: INR 10,139/- per night

Google Rating: 4.2/5 | TripAdvisor Rating: 4.5/5

Destiny The Farm Resort, Tamil Nadu

A perfect mix of adventure and wildlife, Destiny The Farm Resort gives you a taste of the farm life near Ooty. It’s the favourite spot of all kids on a trip to Tamil Nadu where they can play around amid cows, horses, rabbits, and sheep for company. It’s among the best family holiday resorts for those looking for a perfect getaway from the chaos and clutter of city life.

Location: Mulligoor Village, Ooty 643001

Activities For Children: Picnics by the lake, tractor rides, agri-dairy tours, zip-lining, horse riding lessons, bonfire, karaoke

Starting Price: INR 9,354/- per night

Google Rating: 4.3/5 | TripAdvisor Rating: 4.5/5
